Hi, I'm Eylen!

I build and design front-end sites for small businesses and my full-time role at Faithlife — a tech company based in Bellingham, WA. I completed DesignLab’s UX Academy in March 2021, where I learned strong UX/UI principles, researching, testing, prototyping, and more. On top of my formal experience, I love creating art inspired by my Korean and Russian upbringing. It's natural for me to combine my knack for art & design with my knowledge of code to create user-centered digital experiences.

My journey

My journey with UX/UI design began with being self-taught in front-end web development. I was fascinated that my code could turn a prototype into a working product. As a developer, I would stare at other people's prototypes all day. I realized that coding wasn't enough, and that I was itching to use my creativity and understanding of the web to design experiences that people will enjoy, need, and return to because it sparks joy and curiosity.

This realization led me to start learning UX/UI in my free time and more recently at DesignLab. The journey doesn’t end there — I am always on the lookout to learn new things!
